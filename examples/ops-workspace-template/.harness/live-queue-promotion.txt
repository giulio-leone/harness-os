LIVE QUEUE PROMOTION HELPER

This wrapper calls the universal `promote_queue` action from agent-harness-core.

Preview only (no mutation):
  bash .harness/run-live-queue-promotion.sh

Execute real queue promotion on the canonical live DB:
  bash .harness/run-live-queue-promotion.sh --execute

Outputs:
  .harness/runtime/live-queue-promotion-preview.json
  .harness/runtime/live-queue-promotion-result.json
  .harness/runtime/live-queue-promotion-overview.json
